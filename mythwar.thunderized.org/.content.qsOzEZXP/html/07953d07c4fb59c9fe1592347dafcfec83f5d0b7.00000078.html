Shapeshift Potion

~ adopted from p0rnst4r (missouri) Shapeshifting guide

Definition ~ Shapeshifting, transformation or transmogrification is a change in the form or shape of a person, especially: * a change from human form to animal form and vice versa* a change in appearance from one person to another


In Mythwar, players can shapeshift into monster and at the same time gaining special attributes the potion offer. There is a few methods to getting SS pots:
- Buy from Demanco, Blyton 186,520(Glimmer Fish SS Pot only)
- Use gems to dedoct SS pot (in guild HQ)
- Hunt from monster
- Events

Demanco
Located in Blyton (186,520) talk to him to buy glimmer fish SS pot for 200k gold

Decoting SS pot
Go into Guild HQ and talk to Alchemist, Guild HQ 157,149
Choose Dedoct shapeshifting potion
Give him a G1 - G5 gem and you require 1 service point per try
If you succeed you will receive a message decotion of shapeshifting potion successful
Else continue trying. Do note that the fail rate is quite high

Hunting from monster
Firstly you have to shapeshift to hunt another SS potion. Check the list below for the potion ypu have to shapeshift into and the location where the shapeshift potion can be hunted. Simply after shapeshift go to the location and go into battle. After wining if you are lucky you wil receive a message saying you have receive a shapeshift potion. Note that if you are in a party, the chance to get the shapeshift potion is random.
