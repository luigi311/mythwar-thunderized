Errand Quest

~ adopted from luv2kill (Achernar) soulstone guide

Definition: a short trip that is taken in the performance of a necessary task or mission
The Errand quest rewards the player with redeems points. But mainly its purpose is to introduce a player to the town Woodlingor by asking player to traval around woodlingor in search of NPC. Have a nice trip searching for them.

There are 3 types of Errand Quests: 5 runs, 10 runs and 20 runs. As the NPC you have to talk to is random, I will just list the Co-ordinates of each NPC and you can refer to this guide if you can't find the NPC.

Run for 5 times

Where to start: Porter - Woodlingor (434,231)

Reward = 20 Redeem Points
