>> When a pet level, it raises 40 intimacy point.
>> When a player level, then pet set in battle will raise 60 intimacy point
>> A pet gain 1 intimacy for every battle it undegoes
>> A pet drop -50 intimacy point for every time it dies in a battle
>> A pet set in battle will drop -10 intimacy point whenever the player (owner) dies (tele back to woodlingor)
>> Pet intimacy can be increase by 1 per battle or through Lucretia Quest in Guild HQ
>> For Lucretia quest only grade 1 to grade 5 gems can be use.

Steps to Lucretia Quest

1. Set the pet you intended for it to gain intimacy to battle mode.
2. Talk to Lucretia. He will ask you to help him look for gems.
3. Use the Give (alt-g) function and give him the gem.
4. He will thanks you and your pet will gain intimacy depending on the grade of gems given. 1 service point is deducted
5. Repeat step 1 till you reached the intended intimacy required.
TIPS: the Bag of Magic (available in item mall) is twidely popular with player as it offer the most number of g5 gems for the least price.(10 per type)
NOTE: Lucretia Quest can only raise the pet initmacy till 50k. once your pet reaches 50k mark you cant use it to raise intimacy anymore.

Grade 1 Gem - 7 Intimacy
Grade 2 Gem - 14 Intimacy
Grade 3 Gem - 28 Intimacy
Grade 4 Gem - 56 Intimacy
Grade 5 Gem - 112 Intimacy
