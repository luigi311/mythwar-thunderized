Gems

Ever see player beaing dealt 1 damage from monster while you are dealt with hundred to thousand of damage? The answer to their secret is simply, Gems. Gems increases a player resistance depending of what type is being added into their armour. This guide will simply show you the grade and type of armour the gems can be enhanced onto.

Usage of Gems :
- Use to increase Pet intimacy
- Use to concot shapeshift potion
- Use to gain skill point (Maximum 4000 per skill)
- Use to increase resistance of character

How to get Gems:
- From Events
- From Guildwar (Winner)
- From Hurricane
- From Drow Lord
- From Worn Deity
- From Guild Attolment Quest
- From Mythwar Online Item Mall
- From Mining
- From Lottery
- From Miner

Gems can be mine at Demon Square 390 630(Narrow Mine) and Desert City 102 641(Narrow Mine) currently. You Require a Gem Sack and a Pickaxe to start digging. Mining can be done in offline mode. Simply select pickaxe and select USE in inventory when you are at mining fround to start mining. Every minute pass will yield you a gem. Press Use on the pickaxe to stop mining. Talk to Mine Manager and select you wish to identify the gems and give him the gemsack to get your gems. Talk to Mine Manager to leave. Fighting the Miner give you a chance of winning a grade 3 gem.

Gems can be combine to become a higher grade using the NPC Gem Master 76 520 and Gem Rookie 85 530 at Blyton. The lower the grade the higher the chance of failure. Stardust and Perfect Stardust(100%) can be use to increase success rate. If the combination fail, you will get back one of the gem while the other gem will be lost.
The same type of gems being use for combination will result in a higher grade but of the same type. Using different type of gems will result in a random type of higher grade gem.

NOTE: You can only combine using the same grade of gem. Maximum grade achieve is G-11
Gems can be converted into Diamond. The process is inreversible. Converting them into Diamond, instead of rasing one's resistance to spell, it increase the player attribute such as Berserk, Critical and so on. Proceed to Blyton look for NPC Gem Converter 101 548. Give him and he will convert the gem into diamond for you

Gems and Diamond is cast into Equipment at Blyton. Example: To cast into armour and helmet Give the Equipment follow by the gem to the Armour Infuser. The rest of the Equipment is as the name of the npc. A maximum of 3 gems can be cast into the equipment. But if you decide to cast a gem that is of a higher grade that is being casted in the Equipment, it will replace it. example: You have a Armour with 3 x G10 gem cast into it. You can cast a G11 gem into it and it will replace it and become G11, G10, G10.
