Raising Pets Resistance


To increase resistance for pets, you require CRYSTAL +1 +2 +3 +4 or +5. They are sold at Crystal Seller in Blyton (260,460) or by killing Spirits in Demon Square and Desert City Pet shop. You can choose whether you would like to accept or reject the offered resist.

-Pets can add +1 at lvl 30, +2 at lvl 50, +3 at lvl 70. Reborn pet can add +4 at lvl 1-50 and +5 at lvl 1-70
-But if u did not add +1, +2, +3 crystal before your pet reborn then u have to wait till lvl 50 before u can add +1 - +4 and lvl 70 for +5.
-Baia increase Magical (Fire, Ice, Evil, Flash, Death) resist and Melee resist
-Koradji increase Elemental (Choas, Stun, Hypno, Frail, Poison) resist
-Nexo increase Critical, Berserk, Pierce, Combo, Counter and Hit + Dodge
-Maximum resistance for pet is 90. In the case where the total sum of resist is more then 90, the system will just register a maximum of 90 for that particular resistance. (eg. choas resist: (initial resist)30 + (resit gained from usage of crystal) 13 + 13 + 13 + 13 + 13 = 95 system will change it to just 90).

Steps to add resistance

1. Select the pet you wish to add resistance and set it to BATTLE mode.
2. Give the crystal to either nexo,koradji or baia.
3. The NPC will offer you a random resist (pls take a look at the chart below to have an idea which type of resist the npc will offer.
4. If the offered resist is what you intended for you pet accept it. Your pet resistance will increase. You can now use th next grade of crystal.
5. Else reject the offer and repeat step 2 again. Rejecting the offered resist will result in the the crystal to be used up as well.
TIPS: It take a while before the npc offered a different type of resist. (estimate 30sec ++)
NOTE: You have to use +1 before you can use a +2 and so on.

Setting Resistance for Deity/Saint and Evolving

For Deity and Saint, resistance can be set to yur liking at Defiler (Blyton 276,477), 30 for first resist choice 15 for 2nd choice(can't be same resist), will cost 2m gold and 20 reputation points. For cases where there is rate/damage (eg. critical rate/ damage) the points will be set to the first choice (in this case - critical rate).
Note: The Deity undergoing the reseting must not have crystal being used on it before hand,
- Aldrain (Blyton 281,481) allows deity pet to evolve at lvl 67 and 1-67, it costs 1m gold and 50 reputation points.
Evolve is required else your pet cannot undergo reborn.

Steps to Pet Alteration

Pet Alteration simply means the transfer of the initial resistance of the pet in the egg to your current battle pet.
1. Talk to Lienia (Blyton 242,472) Select Yes if you wish to do a pet alteration
2. Set the pet you wish to alter its initial resistance to battle mode.
3. Give the egg using the Give(alt+g) function and Liena wil change the pet (in battle mode) to the initial resist of the pet in the egg.
NOTE: If crystal has been used on the pet, then it need to be remove using a stone of curse before you can carry out pet alteration.
- You can visit the Pets Page to see the initial resist of all pets.
