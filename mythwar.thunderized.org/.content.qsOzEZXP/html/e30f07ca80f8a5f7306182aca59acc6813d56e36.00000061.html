Forging

Defination ~ Process of working metal to the desired shape by impact or pressure from hammers, presses or forging machines. The metal object so produced is termed a forging.

Like in many others online games, Myth War Online offers forging as a skills. Upon learning it, it allows one to forge the desired lvl of armour (he or she had master to).

Steps to be a forger

1. Go to Demon Square to talk to Master Forger.
2. You will be asked which type of forger you wish to be choose one. Note that if you starts learning and decided to forge another equip you will lose watever experience you had gained from the previous forging.
3. Go redeem your ores from Redeem Stewards and vist the armoury or weapon shop to get yur lvl 1 equipments.
4. Go give your intended-to-be-forge equipments and the ores or reagent for your level to the various Helper. (example if you embark to be a shoe forger go look for Shoe Helper)
5. Congrats you had gained 1 forging experience points
