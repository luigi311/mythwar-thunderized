About Pets

What are pets

Pets are important in MythWarOnline as they play a major roles in battle.

Every main character is allowed to keep a maximum of 20 pets.

Pets will always be able to level up faster than the main characters. (it will get twice the exp the char received)

There are many ways a pet can be use depending on its resistance, skill and attributes

Pets Status Screen

Name
When you receive a pet, the name is the default given by the Mythwar dev. You can simply click the box beside the name and press enter after you have type the name you wish to change the name of your pet. Note that space are not allowed.
Species
The class this pet belongs to. There are the Flying, Human, Special, Dragon, Undead, Demon,
Level
The level of you current pet.
Loyalty
The loyalty of your pet. Default is at 100. Whenever is dies during a battle it loses 20 loyalty point, and whenever it level up it change back to 100. Once loyalty reaches 40 and below the pet will not follow your instruction during battle. Pet will not run away even at 0 loyalty. Simply use a loyalty stone to restore to 100.
Health
The Health point of your pet. Current hp / Maximum HP
Mana
The Magic point of your pet. Current MP / Maximum MP
Attack
The Base Damage your pet can deal on the target
Speed
The Speed of your pet. Determins how fast the pet turn come during battle
Exp
The Total exp point your pet has earned at that lvl. Current Exp / Exp needed to level. At the end of the experience box is some green color number. That is the current Growth rate of your pet.
Stats Point
Whenever your pet level you be given 4 stats point. Use the Positive and Negative icon to raise the stats.
Sta
Determins the HP of your pet
Int
Determins the MP of your pet and magical damage (if any)
Str
Determins the Attack rating
Agi
Determins the Speed rating
Battle
Click to choose the pet youwish to use during battle
Follow
Click to let your pet follow behind you while you are moving around Mythwar. Max GR pet will have a shiny effect.
Dismiss
Click to dismiss your pet from your service. Another box will appears to confirm your choice.
Item
Click to allow pet to use HP or MP potion / items
Confirm
After you have set the Stats point over your attributes you need to press confirm before the changes is register
Click to Display the Physical Attribute (Hit rate, Dodge rate, Defense, Melee Resistance, Pierce / Damage, Berserk Rate/Damage, Critical Rate/Damage/Resistance , Combo Hit/Rate, Counter Attack Rate/ Damage)
Click to Display the Magical Attribute (Evil resistance, Flash resistance, Ice resistance, Fire resistance, Drain resistance, Melee Reflect/Damage, Magic Reflect/Damage)
Click to Display Elemental Attributes (Death resistance, Posion resistance, Chaos resistance, Stun resistance, Hypnotize resistance, Frailty resistance)

Pet Battle Selection


Attack
Instruct Pet to perform a melee attack on target
Skills
Instruct Pet to use spells eg. enhance, flash
Bag
Instruct Pet to use an item from your inventory.
Defend
Instruct Pet to protect a party member or other pet. When the pet or member is being targeted with a melee attack, your pet will "jump in" to take the damage instead.
Dodge
Instruct pet to take up defend position, will take less damage from enemies.
Change
Change to another pet at the next turn. A pet in choas, stun and hypno mode cannot be change if you are also in stun choas and hypno mode otherwise, pet can be change by taking up a turn of yours
