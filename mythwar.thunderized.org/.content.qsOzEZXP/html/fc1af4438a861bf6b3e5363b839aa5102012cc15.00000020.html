AODS

~ Armor of Dreams ~
AODs- Basically meaning Armour of Dream, are special armour that enchance one character with superior stats. Anything from adding one with an extra of 20k++ HP to 300 ++ additional of speed. As the name suggest, its one dream to pocess them but then sad to say, great stuff is always rare.
NOTE: AODs can be found in: Events, Hurricane, Treasure Map Hunting (Full Chip) and from armor boxes.
NOTE: AODs can be upgraded at Demon Square 289,557 Dream Crafter NPC
